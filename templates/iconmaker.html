AppIcons

Three simple steps to create icons for your Android and iOS Apps.

Step 1. Choose your platform.

iOS (icon sizes: 180x180, 120x120, 60x60, 152x152, 76x76)

Android (icon sizes: 36x36, 48x48, 72x72, 96x96)

Step 2. Choose the image file to be resized.(Help)

Step 3. Create icons based on your image file.
